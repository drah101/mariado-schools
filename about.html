Our Story & Mission

Shaping the Future Generation Since 2014

From Mukidoma to Mariado

Mariado Schools carries a rich legacy of education in the Arusha region. Formerly known as Mukidoma School, our institution underwent a significant transformation in 2014. This change was more than just a name; it was a commitment to a higher standard of "Academic and Character Excellence." Since then, we have expanded our campus in Usa River to provide a full educational ladder from Nursery to High School.

Our Mission

To provide a holistic education that equips students with superior academic knowledge, practical life skills, and a strong moral compass rooted in Godly values.

Our Vision

To be a leading private institution in Tanzania that produces graduates who are not only intellectually elite but also socially responsible and spiritually grounded.

More Than Academic Excellence

We recognize that the world today requires more than just a certificate. Our approach focuses on four key pillars of development:

Academic

Rigorous testing and intellectual curiosity.

Discipline

Building integrity and self-control.

Social

Learning leadership and community care.

Spiritual

Founding every success on God's guidance.

Founded on Faith

As a Private School affiliated with the Seventh-Day Adventist (SDA) Church, we operate under a high moral code. We believe that true education is the harmonious development of the physical, the mental, and the spiritual powers.

"Academic and Character Excellence with God Ahead"

While we welcome students from all walks of life, we ensure that every child is equipped with the spiritual strength to face the outside world with confidence.
